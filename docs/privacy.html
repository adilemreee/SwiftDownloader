Privacy Policy

SwiftDownloader

Last updated: March 5, 2025

Overview

SwiftDownloader is a macOS download manager that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

We collect NO personal data.

SwiftDownloader operates entirely on your local machine. All download records, settings, and file information are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Download URLs — The URLs you choose to download from
  • File metadata — File names, sizes, categories, and download dates
  • App preferences — Your settings such as download location, theme, and notification preferences (stored via UserDefaults)
  • Download history — Records of your past downloads

Network Access

SwiftDownloader connects to the internet solely to download files from URLs you provide. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is transmitted.

Clipboard Access

If you enable the optional "Clipboard Monitoring" feature in Settings, the app reads URLs from your clipboard to offer quick downloads. This data is processed locally and never transmitted anywhere. This feature is disabled by default.

Spotlight Integration

Downloaded files may be indexed by macOS Spotlight for searchability. This indexing is handled by the operating system and stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

SwiftDownloader does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all download records at any time from within the app (Settings → Advanced → Clear All Downloads). Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

SwiftDownloader does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
