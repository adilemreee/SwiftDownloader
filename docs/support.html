FAQ

How do I enable the Safari extension?

Go to Safari → Settings → Extensions → Enable SwiftDownloader. Downloads from Safari will automatically appear in the app.

Where are my downloads saved?

By default, files are saved to ~/Downloads. You can change this in Settings → General → Download Location.

How do I schedule a download?

Right-click on any download and select "Schedule..." to pick a specific date and time for the download to start automatically.

Can I download multiple files at once?

Yes! Click "Add Download" → "Bulk" to paste multiple URLs (one per line) and download them all at once.

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings → Appearance → Theme and choose between System, Dark, or Light mode.

Is my data collected?

No. SwiftDownloader operates entirely locally. No data is sent to any server. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
